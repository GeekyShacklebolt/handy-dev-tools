Frequently Asked Questions

What is natural sorting?

Natural sorting orders strings with numbers the way humans expect: file1, file2, file10 instead of file1, file10, file2 (which is standard alphabetical order).

Can I sort and deduplicate at the same time?

Yes, you can combine sorting and deduplication in a single operation.

Is the comparison case-sensitive?

By default, sorting is case-sensitive (A before a). You can toggle case-insensitive mode for sorting and deduplication.
