Frequently Asked Questions

What is Lorem Ipsum?

Lorem Ipsum is dummy text used in the printing and typesetting industry since the 1500s. It is derived from a work by Cicero, with words altered to create meaningless but realistic-looking text.

Why use Lorem Ipsum instead of real text?

Lorem Ipsum helps focus on visual design without the distraction of readable content. It has a fairly normal distribution of letters, making it look like real text at a glance.

Can I generate text in other languages?

This tool generates classical Lorem Ipsum text. The generated text maintains Latin-like character distribution suitable for most Western typography testing.
