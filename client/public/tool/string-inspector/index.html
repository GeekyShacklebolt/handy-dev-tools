Frequently Asked Questions

Why does character count differ from byte count?

In UTF-8, ASCII characters use 1 byte, but characters like emoji or CJK characters use 2-4 bytes. A string with 10 characters might be 10-40 bytes depending on content.

What are invisible characters?

Invisible characters include zero-width spaces (U+200B), zero-width joiners (U+200D), soft hyphens (U+00AD), and various Unicode control characters that are not visible but can cause bugs.

How do I find hidden characters in my string?

Paste your text and the inspector will highlight all non-printable, control, and zero-width characters with their Unicode code points and names.
